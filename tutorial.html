How to remove annoying extensions

  1. Go to chrome://extensions and turn on Developer Mode


  2. Navigate to the Inspect views background page (Service Workers don't work)


  3. Click on Inspect views and click Console


  4. Type in 'close (true); alert ("This extension has been quitted")' or 'window.close(true)'

  5. Done! Now it will stay until your device restarts. (Credit to Pro_Gamer213 at iogames.fourum
